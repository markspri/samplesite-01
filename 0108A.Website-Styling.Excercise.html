Springtime in UK

Springtime in Richmond Park is a beautiful time. Even though it can be chilly at times, it is nice to walk my dog Bacon. This site includes some pictures I have taken on these walks.

Sometimes we walk around the neighborhood, other times we make it to the woods. Richmond Park also has some beautiful lakes and ponds.
